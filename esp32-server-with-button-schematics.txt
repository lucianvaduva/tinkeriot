Option 1: Basic LED Circuit (for beginners)
Connections:

ESP32 GPIO 2 → 220Ω Resistor → Anode of LED

Cathode of LED → GND

This is simple, safe, and great for demonstration.

Option 2: Relay-Controlled AC Light (advanced users)
Connections:

GPIO 2 → IN of Relay Module

VCC of Relay Module → 5V

GND of Relay Module → GND
